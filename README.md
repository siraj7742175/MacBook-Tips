 Apple (🍎) 5 MacBook Tricks That Make Life Easier

1. Press ⌘ + Space to open Spotlight and instantly find apps, files, or even do quick calculations.

2. Use ⌘ + Shift + 4 to capture a selected area of your screen.

3. Hit the Space bar on any file to preview it instantly with Quick Look.

4. Organize your workflow with Stage Manager to reduce desktop clutter.

5. Keep your MacBook cool and avoid prolonged overheating to maintain better battery health.
